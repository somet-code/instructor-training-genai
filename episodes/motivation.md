Motivation enhances learning.
Learners may believe that genAI coding tools have already or will soon make it unnecessary to learn to code.
How can we motivate these individuals to keep learning?

Discussion: Consider the responses you have received from the chatbot in the activities we have done so far. Are there any aspects of these responses that you think might be demotivating for learners? 

Discussion: With a partner, discuss the opportunities that exist in a workshop to motivate learners to continue developing their own ability to produce and understand code. What could you say to learners to encourage them to keep developing their own abilities, and to avoid becoming too reliant on genAI to keep making progress? Choose one of your ideas to share with the whole group.
